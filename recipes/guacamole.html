Guacamole

Description

Guacamole with a bit of spice, thanks to the cayenne and cilantro. Served either smooth or chunky.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma (plum) potatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic.
  2. Stir in cayenne pepper. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
